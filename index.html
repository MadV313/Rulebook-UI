SV13 TCG RULEBOOK

1. Linking Your Deck

Before doing anything else, you must link your saved deck to your Discord account. Use:

/linkdeck [your JSON array]

This enables your deck for duels, card collection tracking, buying and trading.

2. Card Collection

All players start with 0 cards. You can earn cards by purchasing packs or receiving admin rewards.

  • /mycards ‚Äî Manage your collection (max: 250 cards)
  • /buycard ‚Äî Purchase a 3-card pack for 3 coins once per day
  • /sellcard ‚Äî Sell up to 5 cards per day
  • /tradecard ‚Äî Select up to 3 to trade with another selected player
  • /duelcard ‚Äî Admin-only card distribution Give/Take a selected card to a selected player
  • /cardpack ‚Äî Admin-only card pack distribution gives a 3-card pack to a selected player
  • return to hub button ‚Äî Returns player to main hub to navigate other pages

  • /tradecard @user ‚Äî Propose a trade of up to 3 cards with a selected player, selected player will be sent a follow up DM

5. Dueling

  • /challenge @player ‚Äî Initiate a duel (must both have linked decks)
  • /practice ‚Äî Start a bot duel (Admins only for now)
  • draw button ‚Äî Player initiates their turn by drawing a card from their deck
  • play button ‚Äî Plays a selected card from the players hand to the field
  • discard button ‚Äî Discards a selected card from the players hand and moves it to their discard pile
  • end turn button ‚Äî Ends the players turn initiating the next players turn
  • return to hub button ‚Äî Returns player to main hub to navigate other pages

Duels are turn-based and use animated card visuals and effects. Each player starts at 200 HP.
